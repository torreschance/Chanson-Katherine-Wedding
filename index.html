Where to stay

We’ve listed our top accommodation recommendations for your convenience, but you’re welcome to explore other hotels or Airbnb options that may better suit your needs.

UNWND Boutique Hotel

‍ UNWND Boutique Hotel Dumaguete offers a unique and vibrant stay in the heart of Dumaguete City. The hotel features a variety of accommodations, including Luxury King rooms, Cabana Huts, and Dormitory Bunk Beds, catering to diverse traveler needs. Guests can enjoy amenities such as an outdoor swimming pool, spa services, and an on-site restaurant and bar. The hotel’s lively atmosphere and attentive staff make it an excellent choice for those seeking both relaxation and adventure in Dumaguete.

Treehive Guest House


Tree Hive Guest House in Dumaguete offers an eco-friendly, budget-friendly stay with a unique design incorporating a century-old Acacia tree. Its container-style architecture blends with nature, creating a peaceful atmosphere. It’s affordable yet comfortable, with dorms, private rooms, and essential amenities like Wi-Fi, a lounge, and 24-hour reception. Conveniently located near Silliman University and city attractions, it’s ideal for travelers seeking a cozy, nature-inspired, and cost-effective stay.

U Pension

U Pension is a budget-friendly accommodation in Dumaguete City, Philippines, offering clean and modern rooms. Guests have praised its friendly and accommodating staff, as well as its convenient location near local attractions. However, some reviews mention that the air conditioning can be loud and that rooms facing the street may experience noise from nearby clubs. Overall, U Pension provides a comfortable stay with essential amenities, making it a suitable choice for travelers seeking affordability and convenience.

The Flying Fish Hostel

The Flying Fish Hostel in Dumaguete City offers a unique blend of sustainable design and comfort, making it an excellent choice for travelers seeking affordable yet stylish accommodations. Located at 32 Hibbard Avenue, the hostel is conveniently situated near local attractions and amenities.

The Henry Resort Dumaguete is a boutique hotel located in the heart of Dumaguete City, Philippines. It offers elegantly styled rooms and well-thought-out amenities, providing guests with a comfortable and unique experience. The resort features an outdoor pool, on-site art gallery, and multiple dining options, including two restaurants and a coffee shop. Its central location allows easy access to local attractions, making it an ideal choice for travelers seeking both relaxation and exploration.
